# Excel-Project
1. Overall Business Performance

The dashboard shows a clear view of total revenue, total orders, total quantity, and number of products.

This helps management quickly understand the company’s overall performance at a glance.

2. Revenue Trend Analysis

The revenue trend chart indicates whether revenues are increasing or decreasing over time.

The general upward movement suggests positive growth and improving business results.

3. Customer & Quantity Growth

The Customer vs. Quantity trend shows steady growth, meaning demand is increasing.

This helps in planning capacity, stock, and operational resources.

4. Regional and Category Insights

Filters for Region and Category allow the manager to identify which regions or product categories contribute the most.

This supports better targeting, marketing focus, and resource allocation.

5. Segment Performance

Revenue by Segment highlights which customer segments (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office) are most profitable.

Helps decide where to invest more or which segment needs improvement.

6. Shipping Mode Effectiveness

The “Highest Ship Mode in Terms of Revenue” shows which shipping method generates most revenue.

Useful for optimizing logistics and improving delivery performance.

7. Top States by Revenue

Identifying the top-performing states helps in strategic expansion, marketing campaigns, and budgeting.
